About us

We are a business specializing in organic, locally grown foods

History

Bountil foods was born in 2015 with the idea of providing healthy food options to people, however the project did not start until the end of 2019, we started in Carlsbad, California with a small food truck that we rented monthly, and our idea was so accepted that a few months later we bought our first own truck and then the business grew, until finally we bought more trucks, expanded our menu, and expanded to different coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego.
