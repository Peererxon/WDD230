Membership Levels

Nonprofit

No fee

Nonprofit membership is for businesses that are not for profit and are not self-employed.

Bronze

$50/month

Bronze membership is for businesses with less than 50 employees that are for profit. Benefits include:

  • ✓ 10% discount on events
  • ✓ 10% discount on advertising
  • ✓ Networking, being a member of the chamber facilitates direct contact with other affiliated companies, through events, thematic forums and training, among others.

Silver

$100/month

Silver membership is for businesses with less than 250 employees that are for profit. This membership includes all the benefits of the bronze membership and :

  • ✓ VIP Service in certain processes of the Commercial Register
  • ✓ Digital signature management for e-commerce

Gold

$140/month

Gold membership is for businesses with more than 250 employees that are for profit. This membership includes all the benefits of silver membership and :

  • ✓ Entries to specialized thematic forums on aspects related to your business activity.
  • ✓ Free talks and training courses for your staff
  • ✓ Training facilities designed according to your company's needs.
  • ✓ Broadcasting of your company's contacts in the annual directory of the Chambers of Commerce of Venezuela; in addition, on the website.
