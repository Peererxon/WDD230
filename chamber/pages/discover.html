Discover Barquisimeto

Geography

Barquisimeto is located on the terrace of the same name, on the banks of the ”Río Turbio”. Barquisimeto is located in the center-west of Venezuela (353 kilometers (219 mi) from Caracas, the capital of the country), which can be traveled in 4 to 5 hours by car.

Demographics

Barquisimeto has an estimated population of 1.972.233 inhabitants and a territory of 81.700 square kilometers, which represents 8,9% of the national surface and 16,5% of the population.

Weather

In Barquisimeto, the warm climate is dominant although it is attenuated due to its altitude at about 650 meters (0,40 mi) above sea level, which makes its climate pleasant to what it should normally be. In Barquisimeto, summers are short, very hot, muggy, dry and cloudy and winters are short, hot, oppressive, wet and mostly cloudy. During the course of the year, the temperature generally varies from 20 °C (68 ºF) to 31 °C (87,8 ºF) and rarely drops below 19 °C (66,2 ºF) or rises above 34 °C (93,2 ºF).

Tourism

On the occasion of its quadricentennial in 1952, the highest obelisk in Venezuela was erected in Barquisimeto, from where you can contemplate its famous sunsets, which have made it called the "City of twilight". Apart from Barquisimeto, which has cultural and musical activities, museums, temples and theaters, it is worth visiting the historic and colonial area of Carora, the relics of El Tocuyo. Quibor with its museums and famous handicrafts and archeology; Sanare, a beautiful town nestled in the Andes, where you can also buy beautiful handicrafts. We can not fail to recommend a visit to Humocaro, Alto and Bajo, the latter with a beautiful Bolivar Square, at the foot of a high mountain and surrounded by beautiful chaguaramos.
