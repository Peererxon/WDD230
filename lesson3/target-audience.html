    • Target Audience

    The Seattle Chamber of Commerce will be used to serve both local businesses and local customers alike. The Chamber wants to see the success of our local economy and we believe that each of these community members go hand-in-hand.

    Businesses will seek out the Chamber of Commerce to help them become successful in the community. Once a member, they will be able to connect and advocate for other local businesses. We believe that the businesses will want to join the Chamber for this reason alone. Attending events, networking, and keeping the pulse, will benefit them in the long run. Businesses looking to join will be serious about growth, activity, participation, and building Seattle to be its best!

    Locals will utilize the Chamber to find businesses to support. Like businesses who join, this will be a great opportunity for them to network with local businesses and their owners. Locals will also be interested in the Chamber because it will help them stay connected to the community and to support new and old businesses alike.
